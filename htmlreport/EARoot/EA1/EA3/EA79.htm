A class that generalises two possible objects that may be part of a process interaction: another process or a data object.  <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>It is assumed that interactions between two data objects would never occur, except through a process, and hence at least one of the participants in all interactions will be process (the predecessor and successor interactionType can be used to distinguish which end in the case of an interaction with a data object at one end).<br /></p>
