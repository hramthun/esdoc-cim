public M4P_InteractionType
  interactionType
Details:
Notes: The type of interaction between the two processes (for one of which this is the configuration), encompassing relationships between scientific processes and other scientific processes, software components and other software components, and workflow related interactions (the most simple of which is the case in which the outputs of one process become inputs into another process).  All of these interactions are part of the configuration, meaning that an unconfigured process can have potential interactions, while the configured process can have actual interactions.
