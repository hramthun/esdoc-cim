An enumeration describing possible types of interaction between two processes, encompassing relationships between scientific processes and other scientific processes, software processes (components) and other software processes (components), and workflow related interactions (the most simple of which is the case in which the outputs of one process become inputs into another process) for either scientific or software processes.  All of these interactions are part of the configuration, meaning that an unconfigured process can have potential interactions, while the configured process can have actual interactions.
