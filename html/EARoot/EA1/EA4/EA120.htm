The role of an association between two processes.  This could encompass different types of association within the same FTC, or associations with processes from another FTC (for example, semantically similar processes). This class is intended to replace (and expand) the FC_InheritanceRelation class, which is limited to an inheritance role.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>This class and its equivalent for properties (M4R_PropertyAssociationRole) are intended to allow relationships to be defined between other properties or processes, of various kinds, with properties and processes from other controlled vocabularies.  ISO 19110 only caters for inheritance relations between feature types in the same FTC, and characterises (attributeOfattribute) relations between properties in the same FTC. However, the representation here requires the referenced controlled vocabularies to also be represented in the form of a FTC.  A preferable approach would be to replace the link to another FTC with a reference to a unique identifier in an ontology or a linked data reference.  <br /></p>
